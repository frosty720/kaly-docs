# KalyBridge

**KalyBridge** is the gateway to the KalyChain ecosystem. It allows users to securely transfer digital assets between KalyChain and other major blockchain networks.

![Bridge Interface](/img/kalyswap/kalyswap_bridge_1765829905488.png)

## Supported Networks

You can currently bridge assets to/from:
*   **Arbitrum (ETH)**
*   **Binance Smart Chain (BSC)**
*   **ClishaChain (CLISHA)**
*   *(More networks coming soon)*

## How to Bridge

1.  Go to the **[Bridge Page](https://app.kalyswap.io/bridge)**.
2.  **Select Direction**: Choose the source chain (e.g., BSC) and the destination chain (KalyChain).
3.  **Choose Asset**: Select the token you want to transfer (e.g., USDT).
4.  **Enter Amount**: Input the amount to transfer.
5.  **Approve**: Allow the bridge contract to access your tokens.
6.  **Transfer**: Confirm the transfer.

### Processing Time
Bridging typically takes **5-15 minutes**, depending on the confirmation times of the source and destination chains.

### Fees
*   **Source Chain Gas**: You pay the standard gas fee on the network you are sending from.
*   **Bridge Fee**: A small fee (typically 0.1%) is deducted to cover the relayers and operations.
