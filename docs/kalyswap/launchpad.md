# Launchpad

The **KalySwap Launchpad** is the premier platform for launching new crypto projects on the KalyChain network. It offers a secure and decentralized way for projects to raise capital and for users to get early access to promising tokens.

![Launchpad Interface](/img/kalyswap/kalyswap_launchpad_1765829913863.png)

## For Investors

Participate in curated presales and fair launches.

### How to Participate
1.  Visit the **[Launchpad Section](https://app.kalyswap.io/launchpad)**.
2.  Browse **"Active"** or **"Upcoming"** sales.
3.  Click on a project to view its details, including whitepaper, tokenomics, and audit status.
4.  Connect your wallet and commit **KLC** (or the required currency) to the sale.
5.  Once the sale concludes, **Claim** your tokens.

## For Developers

Launch your token with ease using our self-service tools.

### Features
*   **Token Creator**: Easily mint standard KRC-20 tokens without coding.
*   **Presale Management**: Set soft/hard caps, start/end times, and whitelists.
*   **Liquidity Locking**: Automatically lock liquidity to build trust with your community.
*   **KYC & Audit Badges**: Showcase your project's legitimacy.

To apply for a verified launch or marketing support, please contact the KalyChain team through our official channels.
