# Welcome to KalyChain

**KalyChain** is a high-performance, EVM-compatible blockchain designed for enterprise and decentralized finance (DeFi) applications. Built on Hyperledger Besu with QBFT consensus, it offers fast finality, low transaction costs, and full Ethereum tooling compatibility.
