# KalyDAO Governance

**KalyDAO** is the decentralized governance body that oversees the KalyChain ecosystem and the KUSD Protocol.

## Overview

Governance is currently managed through community voting and proposal mechanisms, ensuring that the ecosystem remains decentralized and aligned with the interests of its users.

:::info Coming Soon
Detailed documentation on governance processes, voting mechanisms, and proposal submission guidelines is coming soon.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Protocol Parameters**: Adjusting KUSD risk parameters (stability fees, debt ceilings).
- **Collateral Onboarding**: Approving new collateral types for KUSD vaults.
- **Ecosystem Grants**: Allocating funds for development and community growth.
